Project Title: SF Employee Income Analysis

Objectives:
San Francisco is one of the expensive city in the USA. The high salary and house shortage have increased the city dwellers living cost. Recently, the sharp raises of salary has added extra fuel to ramp the cost of living. The San Francisco Controller's Office keeps records the salary information from 2013 to present.

The goal of this project is to get an insight of the SF employee compensation and analyze so that San Francisco Chronicle newspaper can be published as the main page article next month.

To achieve the San Francisco Chronicle newspaper goal, we have to carefully analyze the dataset considering different perspectives. At the beginning of the project, we will get too familiar with the dataset and look into different columns. Then, we will pick the relevant columns that will lead us to reach our goals. The obtained results will be visualized using plot and table.
